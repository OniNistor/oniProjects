Party Builder

Game Rules

Random adventurers will come to visit you!
When you meet an adventurer, you will have two options.
You can RECRUIT them, and they will be added to your party.
Or you can REJECT them to skip to the next adventurer.
You may only RECRUIT a total of 5 times. You may only REJECT a total of 7 times.
So you must decide carefully who to RECRUIT and who to REJECT.
Each adventurer will have a level. Anytime an adventurer is added to your party,
their level is added to the party's overall average level.
The objective is to assembly a party with the highest possible score.
The game will end when you have recruited your fifth member.
GOOD LUCK!
